# Development Roadmap

This roadmap reflects the sequential build order needed to unlock Narrative Intelligence.

---

# **Phase 1 — V0: Analytics Core (Weeks 1–6)**  
- Monorepo + infrastructure  
- Market ingest (Polymarket)  
- Market detail pages  
- Charts + price ticks  
- Watchlist  
- Alerts  
- Cross-venue compare (Polymarket only)  

**Outcome:**  
A fully working Polysights clone and foundation for all future verticals.

---

# **Phase 2 — V2: Truth Feed (Weeks 6–12)**  
- News ingest pipeline  
- Headline → market mapping  
- Δ-probability impact  
- Truth Feed UI  
- Market detail news tab  

**Outcome:**  
Markets become reactive to real-world news.

---

# **Phase 3 — V1: Sentiment Layer (Weeks 12–18)**  
- Poll engine  
- Truth Tokens  
- Region-tagged sentiment  
- Stake-intent weighting  

**Outcome:**  
Crowd sentiment becomes a second signal alongside markets.

---

# **Phase 4 — V3: Localization Engine (Weeks 18–24)**  
- Region profiles  
- Divergence  
- Regional feed  
- “What would you like to see?”  
- Region heatmaps  

**Outcome:**  
Regions reveal their own belief systems.

---

# **Phase 5 — V5: Narrative Intelligence (Weeks 24–36)**  
- Canonical market grouping  
- Composite probability  
- Narrative object  
- Narrative Bond  
- Narrative Feed  
- Narrative Agents (H6)  

**Outcome:**  
The platform evolves from “market viewer” to “Narrative Intelligence Engine”.

---

# **Phase 6 — V4: Exchange Layer (Future)**  
- Local markets  
- Licensing  
- Orderbook  
- Wallets  
- Settlements  

(Not built until compliant.)

---

# Long-Term Vision
Prediction Router becomes:

> **Narrative Intelligence  
> powered by Prediction Data  
> feeding AI Agents.**

With V5 as the core strategic moat.
